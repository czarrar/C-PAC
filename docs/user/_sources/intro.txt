Features

Seed-based Correlation Analysis (SCA)
"""""""""""""""""""""""""""""""""""""
Calculates functional connectivity between a seed region and all other voxels.

Key Paper: `Functional connectivity in the motor cortex of resting human brain using echo-planar MRI. <http://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/8524021>`_

Time-Series Extraction
""""""""""""""""""""""
Extract time-series data for pre-defined units, graph vertices, or each voxel.

Voxel-Matched Homotopic Connectivity (VMHC)
"""""""""""""""""""""""""""""""""""""""""""
Key Paper: `Growing together and growing apart: regional and sex differences in the lifespan developmental trajectories of functional homotopy. <http://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/21068309>`_

Regional Homogeneity (ReHo)
"""""""""""""""""""""""""""
Measures the similarity of the time series of a given voxel to those of its nearest neighbors.

Key Paper: `Regional homogeneity approach to fMRI data analysis. <http://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/15110032>`_ 

Bootstrap Analysis of Stable Clusters (BASC)
""""""""""""""""""""""""""""""""""""""""""""
Quantifies the stability of resting-state networks.

Key Paper: `Multi-level bootstrap analysis of stable clusters in resting-state fMRI. <http://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/20226257>`_

Connectome Wide Association Studies (CWAS)
""""""""""""""""""""""""""""""""""""""""""

Key Resource: `Connectir <http://connectir.projects.nitrc.org/>`_

Fractional Amplitude of Low Frequency Fluctuations
""""""""""""""""""""""""""""""""""""""""""""""""""
Key Paper: `An improved approach to detection of amplitude of low-frequency fluctuation (ALFF) for resting-state fMRI: fractional ALFF. <http://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/18501969>`_

Network Centrality
""""""""""""""""""
Centrality measures quantify functional connectivity within the brain connectome.

Key Paper: `Network centrality in the human functional connectome. <http://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/21968567>`_
